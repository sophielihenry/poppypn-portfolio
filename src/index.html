Poppy Paulus-Nicolas

Poppy Paulus- Nicolas is a multi- disciplinary artist. She often takes the stance of reporter or observer, using her works to weave multi layered narratives. The result is often uncanny, personal & funny. She sees every day moments as riddles to be explained, even if you don't want them explained to you. You can always talk to her about anything and she likes to think of herself as being good with advice. In fact, only yesterday was she driving along the A70 when Jess asked her for an honest opinion on her boyfriend, to which Poppy replied "NASA is still looking for his hairline".
